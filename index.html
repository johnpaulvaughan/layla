LAYLA

Layla helps you love your music today, while supporting your favourite artists of tomorrow.

Layla is a music app that makes it easy to control the music you love and discover new music that fits your unique vibe...

...with 50% of our net profits going back to musicians to make tomorrow sound awesome.

Drop us your email and we'll keep you updated.

Layla connects to your music from cloud services like Spotify, YouTube and Dropbox. You can also import all those songs you have lying around on your hard drive.

Here at Layla we believe the static nature of traditional playlists harm musicians. What about all the amazing songs that don't make the cut.

Layla lets you create bags of songs by combining phrases, artists, albums and songs, mxing them so there's no jarring changes.

Built to party.

You can queue up songbags and set them to transition after a certain time, or even start the transition manually whenever you think the mood needs switching up.

It's seamless and intuitive.

As you swipe songs to ditch or favourite them, Layla learns what you like.

We'll also show you similar songs from both global superstars and the amazing muso around your corner who you didn't know existed.

Music - ethically.

Half of the net profits Layla generates will be funnelled back into enabling musicians to make the music to love tomorrow. You can allocate how your impact will be distributed.
